Essentials Basket

The Essentials Basket will consist of items most commonly forgotten by parents (garnered from our past experience) as well as items to help make admissions in the hospital more comfortable and pleasant.

Each basket will include a food gift card, a parking garage voucher, healthy pre-packaged snacks, and toiletry items.
